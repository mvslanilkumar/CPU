1. Information We Collect GreenBites does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (“Personal Data”) on our servers. However, the App may access or process the following: 1.1 Device Location GreenBites accesses your device’s approximate or precise location only to show nearby restaurants and food options. Location data is processed locally and is never stored by us. 1.2 Restaurant Searches & Queries Your searches or text-input may be sent to: Google Places API (to get business details) Google Gemini API (to generate summaries, menu breakdowns, or answers) OpenStreetMap (to locate restaurants and food venues) We do not receive, store, or share this information. 1.3 Third-Party Services GreenBites integrates with external services that have independent privacy policies: Google Places Google Gemini OpenStreetMap Please review their policies for more details. 2. How We Use Information GreenBites uses the limited data it processes strictly for: Showing restaurants near your location Fetching menus, phone numbers, and business info Providing AI-powered summaries and recommendations Improving search relevance (on-device only) We never sell, store, or share user data. 3. Data Retention GreenBites retains no user data, ever. All queries, locations, and AI interactions are processed in real time and then discarded. 4. Children’s Privacy GreenBites is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. 5. Security Because no personal data is stored on our servers, the risk of data exposure is significantly minimized. All communication with third-party APIs is encrypted via HTTPS. 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.
